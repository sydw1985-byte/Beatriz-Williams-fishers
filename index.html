About

Beatriz Williams

Beatriz Williams is the New York Times, USA Today, and internationally bestselling author of historical novels set among the social and political fault lines of the twentieth century.

Her fiction moves between eras and perspectives, examining the ways ambition, love, and memory shape the lives of women across generations.
