About

Beatriz Williams

Beatriz Williams is the New York Times, USA Today, and internationally bestselling author of historical novels set among the social and political fault lines of the twentieth century.

Her fiction explores ambition, loyalty, inheritance, and the private negotiations of power across generations—often against the luminous backdrop of coastal New England.
